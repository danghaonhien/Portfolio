Trasheroes

Trasheroes is an app that use the power of individual and community to help battle the plastic waste war. The app allows people to earn rewards by picking up and snaping a photo of the plastic waste. It aims to help reduce your communities plastic waste pollution and minimize individuals plastic footprint and to build precised plastic waste data captured by the Trasheroes community.


The Crisis

Plastic pollution is one of the most critical threats for our global environment and we lack of heroes to save our endangered planet.


The Solution

The purpose of Trasheroes is to creatie a platform for people to Connect, Make Impacts, and Earn Rewards . It will help people who are aware of our environment create a community that always want to make an impact and be paid for.

The Power of One

If there is no One, there will be zero progression. Start with One Hero and we can inspire other millions of Heroes to come together to join.

The Power of Community

In order to solve the global environment problem, it requires a community effort. Together, Trasheroes will make an impact.

The Solution

Collecting plastic waste Data points to locate potential Trash Zones. The data will help local government study and research the problem.

The goal is to explore people behavior & habits with plastic waste pollution
